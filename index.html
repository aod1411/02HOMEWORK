About

Aida (Ida) Olvera- Dyckes O.T.R/L, MSOP, CHT, CLT Aida (Ida) is an OT with nearly 30 years’ experience She is a graduate of EMU and holds a B.S. in Occupational Therapy, After several years she returned to EMU to obtain her Master’s degree of Science in prosthetics and orthotics. She has worked in several states which has afforded her to provide our clients with a diverse portfolio with experiences in Neurological patients, pediatrics and has particular expertise in the area of hand therapy she not only is NDT certified she holds two advance credentials as a Certified hand therapist and a Certified Lymphedema therapist. Currently Ida is in the process of changing careers. As a clinician of over 30 years. The struggles of on-line documentation is an all too familiar frustration. The hope is that one day to become part of the solution.
